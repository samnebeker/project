How to Shoot a Basketball

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Balance
    • Keep feet shoulder-width apart with the food on the shooting side of the body toe-to-heel in front of the other foot
    • Knees bent
    • Hips square to target
    • Back straight
  2. Step 2: Elbow In
    • The ball is held close to the chest and underneath the chin with the shooting hand a little more toward the back and slightly under the ball
    • The other hand is placed on the side of the ball and used only as a guide
  3. Step 3: Eyes on Target
    • Focus on a target, usually the front of the rim
    • Stay focused on the taget even after the release
  4. Step 4: Follow Through
    • The ball is released by a quick extension of the elbow and a flick of the wrist and fingers of the shooting hand
    • The trick is to think like you are putting your hand in a cookie jar
    • Ball should have back-spin rotation which will enable the shooter to develop a softer touch
